MINDFULNESS

Mindfulness is paying attention in a particular way

On purpose, in the present moment and nonjudgmentally

Mindful awareness is being aware and turned in to what is going on right now, in the present moment. Meditation, in contrast, can be viewed as an approach or practice which can help to cultivate mindful awareness. There are many meditation techniques a person can try.
